Hi, I'm Ramya Raj, a MERN stack developer with a passion for building full-stack web applications.

I'm a passionate front-end developer with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js. I enjoy creating clean, responsive, and user-friendly web interfaces that deliver great user experiences. As a fresher, I'm eager to contribute to real-world projects, continuously learn new technologies, and grow in the field of web development. My focus is on writing clean code, building intuitive designs, and turning ideas into interactive websites.I believe that the best way to solve problems is to break them down into manageable chunks. I always start by understanding the user requirements and project goals thoroughly. Then, I move to the planning stage, where I break down the project into smaller tasks to ensure everything is delivered on time. Once I have a clear roadmap, I start building the front-end, ensuring that it's both user-friendly and responsive. After that, I focus on developing the back-end and connecting it with the front-end using APIs or other necessary integrations.
